Intrusive Meetups

Price: Free!

Intrusive Meetups are great opportunities for high school students with keen interests in the fields of cybersecurity to come and meet like-minded people! While our Meetups are great social events, we also provide the opportunity for you to learn new things, as well as for you to also spread YOUR knowledge to others! Whether you are a seasoned infosec guru or a newbie amateur, Intrusive does not discriminate you from speaking and presenting at our events. If you wish to speak and conduct a presentation at our next event, please do not hesistate to email hello@intrusive.tech, with your name, age, school, brief description of talk, and the presentation itself.

hack[]

Price: Free!

Intrusive's hack[] program is an upcoming cybersecurity initiative where we partner up with local schools to help start cybersecurity and computer-science clubs and programs for underserved students. hack[] programs are equipped with project-based curriculums and all the necessary resources that allow students to take a step outside of the conventional classroom and build upon their knowledge in the STEM fields. If you are an educator or an interested student, please email hello@intrusive.tech.
